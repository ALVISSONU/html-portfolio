The Best Novel According to Alvis

My top 3 Novel of all-time.

  • The Power of your subconscious mind

    • The central point of the book is that you can use your imagination to suggest ideas to your subconscious to get what you want.

  • Rich Dad Poor Dad

    • Throughout the book, the author explains how a person can increase their wealth by investing in assets and by being smart with money and focuses on the importance of financial literacy from an early age

  • To Kill a Mockingbird

    • The most important theme of To Kill a Mockingbird is the book's exploration of the moral nature of human beings that is, whether people are essentially good or essentially evil.
